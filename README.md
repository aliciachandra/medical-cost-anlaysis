# Medical Insurance Cost Analysis

Analysing how demographic and lifestyle factors drive medical insurance 
charges using a dataset of 1,338 policyholders.

**Tools used:** Python, pandas, matplotlib  
**Data source:** Medical Insurance dataset

## Key findings
- Smokers pay significantly higher annual charges than non-smokers
- Insurance charges increase steadily with age
- High BMI combined with smoking produces the most extreme charges
- Lifestyle factors are stronger cost drivers than regional variation
